A little about us

We're a
neighbourhood salon.

The Bloom Room has been part of this community for over 15 years. We're not a chain — we're a small, independent team that genuinely cares about every person who walks through the door.

We give honest advice, we don't upsell, and we'll always tell you if something won't work for your hair. Friendliness isn't something we put on — it's just how we are.
