What we do

  1. 01

    Accident Repair

    From minor bumps to serious structural damage. We work directly with all major insurers.

  2. 02

    Paint Respray

    Full resprays and panel-by-panel colour matching. Cellulose, basecoat/clearcoat, matte.

  3. 03

    Panel Beating

    Dents pulled and panels reshaped by hand before any filler or paint touches the car.

  4. 04

    Alloy Refurbishment

    Kerbed, corroded, or cracked alloys stripped and powder-coated or diamond-cut.

  5. 05

    MOT Preparation

    We find what'll fail before the test does. Bodywork, lights, structural checks.

  6. 06

    Welding

    MIG and TIG. Sill repairs, chassis work, custom fabrication. Tested and certified.

  7. 07

    SMART Repairs

    Small to Medium Area Repair Technology. Scratches, chips, and scuffs fixed without a full respray.
